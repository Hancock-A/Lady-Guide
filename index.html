Welcome to Lady's Manor

A Guide to Caring for The Puss

You have been selected for a most distinguished honor: the care and keeping of Lady, a six-year-old orange starlet who identifies as a "Snuggly Roast Chicken." She is the director, the producer, and the lead actress of this apartment. This guide shall ensure her performance reviews remain glowing.

🐈 The Protagonist: Lady

📸 The Daily Photo Call

Please send daily dispatches of Lady via Facebook Messenger. We shall be aboard a yacht, and the cellular signal is quite "unpredictable," but Messenger shall find us!

🍽️ The Feeding Rituals

Lady expects her meals to arrive with the punctuality of a Swiss watch and the presentation of a Michelin-starred establishment.

Morning ½ can Fancy Feast or 1 Petites tray
Afternoon Tea (3:30-4:00 PM) ½ can Fancy Feast or 1 Petites tray
Evening (5:00-6:00 PM) ½ can Fancy Feast or 1 Petites tray
Nightcap A delicate scoop of 'Night Time' kibble
Note: Use a fresh plate for every wet food performance. Silicon covers for opened cans are in the coffee drawer. Simply rinse used plates and place in the lower rack of the dishwasher.

🎭 The Playbill

Lady will play when she decides. She enjoys chasing the red laser dot or a crumpled post-it note (she will dance for one on a string!).

Her favorite co-star is Charmaine the Mouse. To keep the magic alive, we hide Charmaine in the fridge so she feels like a "new" discovery each time.

🧻 The Lady Lounge (Litter Management)

"While Lady is the epitome of grace, even a star has… obligations. We must ensure her powder room remains pristine, lest she stage a protest."

The Daily Performance

Please attend to the litter box daily (or every other day, if you must). Use the scoop to remove any "clumps or nuggets" of delight.

The Exiling of Parcels

Place the findings into a plastic bag. We find it best to empty the kitchen trash daily and simply stop by the lady lounge on the way to the trash room.

The Trash Room

Once bagged, take the refuse down the hall to the Trash Room. It's a short walk for you, but a giant leap for the aroma of the manor.

Supplies: Fresh kitchen bin bags are located in the office closet, directly in front of you as you enter.

The Aftermath

Should Lady be over-enthusiastic making circles in the sand, feel free to utilize the Hoover Stick Vac in the office closet to restore order.

🏛️ The Manor: The Domestic Arts

A comprehensive guide to the various mechanical wonders within these walls.

📶 WiFi Information

📺Roku TV

The Power

Press the red button. It's the "Open Sesame" of our era.

The Navigation

The Home button (the house icon) is your anchor. Press it to see the grid of apps. Use the purple Directional Pad to move through the selections and the center OK button to dive in.

The Volume (The Secret!)

The volume buttons are located on the right side of the remote. There is an Up, a Down, and a Mute. They are quite tactical, yet easy to miss!

📺 Available Subscriptions

All accounts are logged in and ready for your viewing pleasure:

  • Netflix
  • Prime Video
  • Britbox
  • Hulu
  • Sling (CNN, HGTV, etc)

Local Lore

Select the "Live TV" tile or use shortcut "1" for local CBS, ABC, and NBC.

📝 Closed Captions (CC)

Press "2" for closed captions, or the "*" (Star) button for a menu during the show.

If that fails, click the Down Arrow during playback—it varies by app, which is simply disappointing, but persistence is key!

☕Espresso

Basic Operation

  1. Ensure the water tank is full and there are beans in the hopper
  2. Place your mug under the spout
  3. Press the espresso button
  4. Await your liquid motivation
Maintenance: If a red light blinks, the machine is parched or full. Empty the grounds bin and drip tray.

☕Milky Drinks

Using the Milk Carafe

  1. Add only the milk you need for one drink into the carafe
  2. Attach the carafe to the machine
  3. Press your desired milky beverage button
  4. Brew and enjoy
  5. Immediately rinse the carafe after use
Important: To avoid disappointment when it's time for your next hot beverage, rinse after each use.

🧺Washer

  1. Place liquid detergent directly in the drum
  2. Load your laundry
  3. Set to 'Permanent Press' or 'Heavy Duty'
  4. Press Start

🧺Dryer

  1. Clean the lint screen before every load
  2. Transfer clothes from washer
  3. Set to 'Easy Care' or 'Cottons'
  4. Press Start

🍽️Dishwasher

Pods are located in the box under the sink.

  1. Load dishes (Lady's plates go on the lower rack)
  2. Add a detergent pod to the dispenser
  3. Close the door
  4. Press 'Start'

⚠️ Crucial Detail

If you open the door mid-cycle, you must press 'Start' again, or the machine will simply give up on life.

🧹The Hoover (The Fur-Finder)

Location: Office closet, mounted on the wall

Operation

  1. Lift off the wall mount
  2. Press the 'On' button
  3. Vacuum as needed
  4. Press 'Off' when finished

Emptying the Tank

Press the tank release button. Empty contents into trash.

Charging

Return the vacuum to its wall-mounted perch; It will charge automatically.

⚠️ The Rules

For the safety and sanity of all involved, please observe these sacred decrees.

🚪 The Forbidden Closets

Keep the laundry and office closets closed at all times.

We don't want Lady "folding" herself into the washer or staging a supply closet intervention.

🌿 The Balcony

Supervised visits only!

If she looks like she's preparing for a daring leap (the 'pre-pounce' stance), the show is over. Close the door immediately.

Lady is an indoor cat with delusions of parkour grandeur.

🚫 The Forbidden List

Items and areas that are strictly off-limits to Lady:

  • Closets: Laundry and office closets must remain closed
  • Unsupervised Balcony Access: She's convinced she can fly
  • Human Food: Lady's dietary requirements are strict and fancy
  • Plastic Bags: A choking hazard disguised as a toy

🚪 The Exit: Departure Day

As all good things must come to an end, here is your graceful departure checklist.

Final Walkthrough

  • Lady has been fed her morning meal
  • Fresh water is available
  • Windows and balcony door are closed
  • Laundry and office closets are closed
